Easy Meatloaf

Description

This is a quick and easy recipe for a meatloaf. It will not take long to make all.

Making this meatloaf is only going to require a few ingredients and just only has four steps to follow.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons prepared mustard
  • 1/3 cup ketchup

Steps

  1. Step 1- Preheat oven 350 degrees F (175 degree C)
  2. Step 2- In a large bowl, combine the beef,egg, onion, milk and bread or cracker cumbs. Season with salt and pepper to tast and place in a lightly greased 9 x 5 inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9 x 13 inch baking dish.
  3. Step 3- In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over meatloaf.
  4. Step 4- Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C ) for 1 hour
