Sweet Slow Cooker Ham

Description

This ham is so easy to put together and cook in a slow cooker, and with a meat thermometer you can't go wrong! The juices can be thickened with cornstarch at the end to make a glaze.

Ingredients
  • 1 (16 ounce ) package light brown sugar, divided
  • 1 (6 to 7 pound ) bone-in picnic ham, rinsed
  • 1 ( 20 ounce ) can crushed pineapple
  • 3/4 cup chai tea latte concentrate ( such as Tazo)

Steps
  1. Step 1- Spread about 3/4 of the brown sugar into the bottom of the crock of your slow cooker to cover completely.
  2. Step 2-Place the on top of the brown sugar with the flat side facing down.
  3. Step 3-Pour pineapple over ham.
  4. Step 4-Rub remaining brown sugar over ham.
  5. Step 5-Pour chai tea latte concentrate over the ham.
  6. Step 6-Place cover over slow cooker and cook on Low until and instant read thermometer inserted into the ceneter of the ham reads at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C), about 5 1/2 hours.
  7. Step 7-Baste ham once or twice with juices while cooking.
